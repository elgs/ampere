Ampere

Smart battery management for your Mac. Monitor, control, and extend your battery's lifespan from the menu bar.

Install with Homebrew brew tap az-code-lab/taps && brew install --cask ampere

Features

⚡

Real-Time Battery Stats

Percentage, cycle count, health, temperature, voltage, amperage, wattage, capacity, and battery age — all at a glance.

⏸

Charge Control

Pause and resume charging with a single click. Manual or automatic — you choose.

🎯

Auto Charge Management

Set upper and lower bounds. Ampere keeps your battery in the sweet spot automatically.

🛡

Micro-Charge Prevention

Prevents short charge cycles between bounds. Only charges from below the lower bound or on your explicit request.

🔋

Active Discharge

Optionally drain to your target level on AC power instead of waiting for passive drain under load.

🛡

Health Check

Periodically verifies SMC state matches expected values. Alerts you if something is off.

📌

Pinnable Panel

Pin the popover open while you work. It stays visible even when you click elsewhere.

🔒

Safe by Design

All SMC overrides are cleared on quit. A watchdog daemon cleans up even after a crash or force-kill.

🔄

Update Notifications

Checks for new versions automatically and shows an icon when an update is available.

How It Works

1

Install

One command with Homebrew, or download the DMG from GitHub Releases.

2

Set Your Bounds

Choose your ideal charge range with the slider. Common recommendation: 40% – 80%.

3

Forget About It

Ampere manages charging automatically. Launch at login and it works silently in your menu bar.

Requirements

macOS 14 Sonoma or later Apple Silicon Mac Admin privileges for charge control
